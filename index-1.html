The current situation of stray cats

In the streets and alleys of the city, countless stray cats are struggling to survive. They face multiple challenges such as food shortages, bad weather, diseases and human indifference. Many cats lose their lives because they are not rescued in time. They are innocent, but they have to endure pain that does not belong to them.

about Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to improving the lives of stray cats. Our team is composed of a group of animal-loving volunteers who are committed to providing food, medical care and shelter for stray cats, and raising public awareness of stray animal issues through various activities.

Since its establishment, we have helped hundreds of stray cats find warm homes and effectively controlled the number of stray cats through sterilization programs. We believe that every life deserves respect and love.

How to Help

  • Adopt instead of buying：Consider adopting a stray cat from a rescue organization or the street and giving them a loving home.
  • Providing temporary shelter：If you can't keep a cat permanently, you can provide a temporary safe haven for stray cats.
  • Participate in TNR Program：TNR (trap-neuter-release) is an effective way to control the number of stray cats.
  • Donate Materials：Donate cat food, cat litter, cat beds and other supplies to rescue organizations.
  • Volunteering：Join a local animal rescue organization, become a volunteer, and help more stray cats.
