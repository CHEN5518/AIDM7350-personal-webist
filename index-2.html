Let every stray cat find a warm home

In this world, there are countless stray cats wandering on the streets, they are longing for love and a warm home. We believe that through our joint efforts, we can create a better future for them.

Material Donation

Supplies we need

  • Cat food (adult cat food, kitten food)
  • Cat litter
  • Cat bed
  • Cat Toys
  • Medical supplies (medicines, bandages, etc.)
  • Cleaning supplies (disinfectant, towels, etc.)
  • His cat daily necessities

Ways to Donate

You can choose any of the following ways to donate supplies:

  • Send the supplies directly to our relief center: No. 123, Futian Port, Shenzhen, Guangdong
  • Send materials by express delivery (please contact us first to confirm the recipient information)
  • Purchase designated supplies in our online store and we will receive your donation directly
